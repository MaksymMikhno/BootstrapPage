History of Perth Road

Perth Road is one of Dundee’s most historic and lively streets, known for amount of lovely places, which are encouraging creativity and enjoyment. It has been a central part of the city’s culture and development for centuries, attracting students, artists, and professionals alike.

The road is home to historic buildings, local businesses, and cultural landmarks, making it a key part of Dundee’s identity.
